Who we are

Business enthusiast, Innovator, & Collaborator

Led by Dydo Flores, Michelle Santos Flores, and John Mathew Eskabons. We are a business enthusiast people who also enters different business such as liquor shop, Digital printing, and also Japanese translation services.

A team of passionate business enthusiasts, we have embarked on various entrepreneurial ventures. One such venture is our successful liquor shop, where we curate a wide range of spirits to cater to the diverse tastes of our customers. With our keen understanding of the market and dedication to customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation for offering high-quality products and exceptional service. In addition to our liquor shop, we have also ventured into the world of digital printing. Recognizing the growing demand for personalized and customized printing services, we have established a state-of-the-art digital printing facility.
Furthermore, our team has tapped into the ever-expanding market for language services by offering Japanese translation services. With the rise in globalization and cultural exchange, we understand the importance of effective communication. Driven by our passion for business and fueled by our commitment to excellence, we strive to create successful ventures that add value to the lives of our customers. With our liquor shop, digital printing services, and Japanese translation expertise, we continue to explore new opportunities and contribute to the growth of various industries.

Our Team

Dydo Flores is driven by the passion for his hobby and envisions his liquor business as a destination that celebrates the artistry, diversity, and enjoyment of fine beverages. Through his dedication, commitment to quality, and a customer-centric approach, he aims to establish a successful enterprise that becomes a trusted name in the liquor industry.

Michelle Flores aims to contribute to a world where language barriers are overcome, cross-cultural understanding is fostered, and accurate interpretation is accessible to all. With her annotation and Japanese translation skills, she aspires to empower individuals, businesses, and communities to communicate effectively across languages and embrace the richness of diverse cultures.

John Mathew Eslabon aims to create a business that delivers high-quality digital printing services on custom t-shirts while offering reliable and professional aircon cleaning solutions. With a focus on personalized creativity, exceptional customer satisfaction, continuous improvement, and sustainability, he envisions building a successful venture that leaves a positive impact on his customers and the environment.
