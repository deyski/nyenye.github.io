Whiskey

ALL ABOUT WHISKY

Whiskey is a popular distilled alcoholic beverage with a long history and diverse variations enjoyed globally. It includes types such as Scotch, Irish, Bourbon, and Rye, each offering unique flavors and characteristics. Whiskey is made from fermented grains, distilled, and aged in wooden barrels to develop complexity. The aging process adds flavors and colors to the spirit. Whiskey can have notes of caramel, vanilla, smoke, fruit, and spice. It can be enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in various cocktails. Whiskey has a rich cultural significance, with enthusiasts appreciating its craftsmanship and attending tastings. Responsible consumption is advised
